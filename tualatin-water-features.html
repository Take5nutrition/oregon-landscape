Common Questions

Water Features in Tualatin — FAQ

Are there setback requirements for water features near the Tualatin River?

Yes — properties near the Tualatin River and its tributaries have riparian buffer zones that can affect where we excavate and what we install. We check applicable setbacks before siting any water feature near the river corridor.

What types of water features suit Tualatin's suburban neighborhoods?

Tualatin's established neighborhoods with mature trees and moderate lot sizes are well-suited for pondless waterfall systems, naturalistic streams, and smaller formal pond installations. We match the scale and style of the feature to the character of the property.

Does the Tualatin area have HOA restrictions on water features?

Some Tualatin neighborhoods, particularly newer HOA communities, have restrictions on mechanical equipment noise levels and water feature visibility from the street. We review HOA requirements before design and spec quiet, efficient equipment.
