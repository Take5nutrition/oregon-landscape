Our Process

No Surprises. No Guessing. Just a Clear Path Forward.

One of the biggest reasons people hesitate to start a landscape project is not knowing what they're getting into. How long will it take? What decisions will we have to make? What if we change our minds?

We've designed our process to answer those questions before you ever sign anything. Every step is clearly defined, every decision is made together, and nothing moves forward until you're completely confident in what we're building.

01
Step One

Free Consultation

It starts with a conversation. Call us, fill out the contact form, or send an email — and we'll set up a time to meet you at your property. There's no cost and no commitment.

We walk the site together, listen to what you're hoping to create, and get a clear picture of your goals, timeline, and budget. This isn't a sales call — it's us getting to know your project so we can give you a real assessment of what's possible.

02
Step Two

Site Assessment & Custom Design

After the consultation, our design team goes to work. We document your property — measuring grades, assessing drainage, mapping sun and shade patterns, and evaluating soil conditions. We don't design around an idea of your yard; we design around the actual yard.

From there we build a custom landscape plan that integrates every element — hardscape, planting, water, lighting, and irrigation — into a unified design drawn specifically for your property. Nothing is copied from a catalog.

03
Step Three

Design Review & Proposal

We present the design to you in detail — walking through every element, explaining the reasoning behind each decision, and showing you how it all connects. This is a real conversation, not a slide deck. If something doesn't feel right, we adjust it.

Once the design is dialed in, we put together a complete written proposal with an itemized scope of work, material specifications, total cost, and a projected start date. You'll know exactly what you're getting and exactly what it costs — no vague estimates.

04
Step Four

Installation

Once the contract is signed and the start date is confirmed, our in-house crew gets to work. We never use subcontractors — the same team that helped design your project is the team building it. That means consistent quality and clear accountability from day one to day done.

We work in a logical sequence — grading and drainage first, then hardscape structure, then planting, irrigation, and lighting — so each phase supports the next. We keep your property clean and communicate with you throughout the build.

05
Step Five

Final Walkthrough & Handoff

When the work is complete, we do a thorough walkthrough with you — inspecting every element, testing every system, and making sure every detail matches the plan we agreed on. We're not done until you say you're satisfied.

We also walk you through how everything works: how to operate your irrigation controller, how to adjust your lighting zones, and what your seasonal maintenance schedule looks like. You'll leave the walkthrough knowing exactly how to care for your new landscape.

06
Step Six — Optional

Ongoing Maintenance

Your landscape will look its best on day one. Keeping it that way takes the right care at the right times of year. We offer ongoing maintenance programs for the landscapes we build — seasonal pruning, mulching, irrigation adjustments, and spring and fall cleanups handled by the same crew that built it.

Maintenance clients get priority scheduling and the peace of mind that comes with having a crew who already knows your property intimately. It's the easiest way to protect the investment you made in your landscape.

Workmanship Warranty

Every installation is backed by our workmanship guarantee. If something we built fails due to installation error, we come back and make it right — no questions asked.

No Subcontractors

Our in-house crew handles every phase of your project. You always know who's on your property and who's accountable for the work.

Clear Communication

You'll always know where your project stands. We update you at every phase and give you a direct line to your project lead throughout.

Firm Timelines

We give you a projected start and completion window before we begin — and we hold to it. Your time matters as much as your project does.
