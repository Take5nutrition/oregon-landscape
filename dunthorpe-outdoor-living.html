Common Questions

Outdoor Living in Dunthorpe — FAQ

What scale of outdoor living space is typical in Dunthorpe?

Dunthorpe properties often support full estate-scale outdoor rooms — covered dining and living areas, full outdoor kitchens, fire features, and integrated lighting across multiple connected spaces. The lot sizes allow for ambition in design.

What premium materials are appropriate for Dunthorpe outdoor structures?

Dunthorpe projects typically call for old-growth cedar or Ipe for covered structures, natural stone for countertops and flooring, and custom ironwork for accent elements. We source materials that match the quality standard of the surrounding property.

How does an outdoor living space integrate with Dunthorpe's mature landscape?

The best Dunthorpe outdoor rooms work with the existing tree canopy and mature planting rather than against it — using shade from established trees, framing views through the landscape, and anchoring the design to features that have been there for decades.
