Common Questions

Water Features in Milwaukie — FAQ

Can a meaningful water feature fit on a smaller Milwaukie lot?

Absolutely — pondless waterfall systems and compact fountain features deliver the sound and visual appeal of moving water in a footprint that works on urban-scale lots. Some of our most creatively designed water features are on small properties.

Does Milwaukie's proximity to the Willamette affect water table depth?

Some lower-lying Milwaukie properties near the river have shallower water tables that affect excavation depth. We assess site conditions before committing to pond depth specifications.

Do Milwaukie water features require city permits?

Most residential water features in Milwaukie don't require permits, but significant excavation or any work in the right-of-way does. We check applicable requirements before breaking ground so there are no unexpected delays.
