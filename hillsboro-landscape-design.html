Common Questions

Landscape Design in Hillsboro — FAQ

What's the typical starting point for Hillsboro landscaping projects?

Many Hillsboro clients are in newer developments where builder landscaping — thin sod, minimal beds, no design intent — is the starting point. We frequently begin with soil correction, proper drainage, and a design built for the actual property rather than what was cheapest to install.

Which permitting authority applies in Hillsboro?

Within Hillsboro city limits, the City of Hillsboro handles permits. Unincorporated areas nearby fall under Washington County. We determine which applies to your property and manage the permit process so you don't have to navigate it yourself.

What's the client base like in Hillsboro?

Hillsboro has a large professional demographic connected to the semiconductor and tech industries — clients who tend to have specific ideas, a design eye, and an expectation of quality that matches their investment. We enjoy working with clients who are engaged in the design process.
