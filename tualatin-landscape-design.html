Common Questions

Landscape Design in Tualatin — FAQ

Are there stream buffer requirements near the Tualatin River?

Yes — properties near the Tualatin River and its tributaries are subject to riparian buffer requirements that can limit grading, impervious surface, and certain plantings. We check these regulations for every property near the river before starting design.

How do HOA standards affect design in Tualatin?

Many Tualatin neighborhoods, especially newer ones, have HOA guidelines covering plant species, fence materials, and hardscape finishes. We review HOA documents before design and build compliance in from the start rather than adjusting after the fact.

Does Tualatin fall under Washington County or its own city rules?

Tualatin is an incorporated city with its own zoning and permitting, though some adjacent areas fall under Washington County. We determine which jurisdiction applies to your parcel and manage the permit process accordingly.
