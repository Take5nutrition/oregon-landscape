Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 26, 2025

Oregon Landscape LLC ("Oregon Landscape," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our website or contact us about our services.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Your name, phone number, and email address when you submit a contact or estimate request form
  • Your property address or general location when relevant to a service inquiry
  • Any project details or notes you choose to include in a message

We also collect limited information automatically when you visit our website, including your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent on the site. This information is collected through standard web analytics tools and is not linked to your personal identity.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and schedule consultations
  • Provide estimates, proposals, and project-related communications
  • Follow up on past service requests or ongoing projects
  • Improve our website and understand how visitors use it

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Communications

When you contact us, we may follow up by phone, email, or text message to discuss your project. You can opt out of follow-up communications at any time by replying "stop" to a text message or by emailing us at info@oregonlandscape.com.

Cookies and Analytics

Our website may use cookies and third-party analytics services (such as Google Analytics) to understand traffic patterns and improve user experience. These tools collect anonymized data and do not identify you personally. You can disable cookies through your browser settings at any time.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information you share with us. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites (such as review platforms or social media). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their policies separately.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of our website after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
