Common Questions

Landscape Design in Lake Oswego — FAQ

Does Lake Oswego have strict tree protection rules?

Yes — Lake Oswego has one of Oregon's most comprehensive tree protection ordinances. Trees above a certain caliper require removal permits, and we factor protected trees into every design from day one to avoid costly surprises mid-project.

How do HOA standards affect landscape design in Lake Oswego?

Many LO neighborhoods, especially around the lake and in newer subdivisions, have HOA guidelines covering plant species, fence heights, and hardscape materials. We're familiar with most of them and design to comply from the start.

What makes lakefront landscape design different?

Lakefront properties require sensitivity around shoreline buffers, surface water runoff, and what's visible from the water. We design with those constraints built in while maximizing the visual and physical connection between the home and the lake.
