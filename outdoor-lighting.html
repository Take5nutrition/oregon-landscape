Outdoor Lighting

Architectural and accent lighting designed into the landscape — so your yard looks just as beautiful after dark.

Landscape Lighting

Lighting Designed Into the Landscape, Not Added After

The best outdoor lighting is invisible during the day and transformative at night. Oregon Landscape plans and installs low-voltage LED lighting systems that are integrated into the landscape design from the start — fixtures buried cleanly, wire runs hidden, and every beam angle chosen for effect.

We design lighting systems for safety and security, for aesthetics and ambiance, and for extending the hours you can enjoy your outdoor space. Whether you need simple path lights along a walkway or a full architectural lighting plan for trees, walls, and focal points, we build it right.

All systems are low-voltage LED — energy-efficient, long-lasting, and compatible with smart home and timer controls.

Lighting Types

What We Install

Path & Walkway Lighting

Low-profile path lights that illuminate walkways and driveways — guiding movement safely through the landscape without harsh glare.

Spotlights & Up-Lighting

Directional spotlights angled to up-light trees, architectural features, and focal points — creating dramatic depth and shadow effects after dark.

Security & Area Lighting

Motion-activated and perimeter lighting that deters intruders and provides broad coverage for dark areas around the property.

Step & Wall Lighting

Recessed step lights and wall-wash fixtures built into hardscape — functional safety lighting that also highlights the craftsmanship of your walls and steps.

Smart Controls & Timers

Low-voltage transformers with programmable timers, dusk-to-dawn sensors, and smart home integration via Wi-Fi or Bluetooth — control your lighting from anywhere.

Repairs & System Upgrades

Existing lighting not performing? We repair broken fixtures, replace failing transformers, and upgrade older halogen systems to modern LED for better light output and lower energy costs.
