About Oregon Landscape

Owner-operated since 2009. Built on craftsmanship, honesty, and a genuine commitment to your property.

Our Story

Rooted in Oregon. Committed to Excellence.

Oregon Landscape was founded in 2009 by three partners who shared a belief that landscaping should be done right — with quality materials, skilled craftsmanship, and a genuine investment in every client's property.

Owners Tony Iranshad, Mike Myer, and Jason Booth bring over 40 years of combined experience across design, installation, and maintenance. They are hands-on in every project, from the first site visit through the final walkthrough.

Based in Clackamas and serving the greater Portland metro area, Oregon Landscape is a company built on reputation — the kind that only comes from doing good work, consistently, year after year.
