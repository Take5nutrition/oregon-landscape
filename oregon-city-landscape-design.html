Common Questions

Landscape Design in Oregon City — FAQ

Does Oregon City's terrain create unusual challenges?

Oregon City sits on a Willamette River bluff with some of the most pronounced elevation changes in the metro area. Many properties involve significant slope, which we address through terracing, engineered retaining walls, and drainage systems that work with the grade.

Are there historic district requirements for landscaping near downtown?

Oregon City has a National Historic District covering parts of downtown and the McLoughlin neighborhood. Landscape changes within historic boundaries can be subject to design review. We check applicable overlays before beginning design.

What's the range of lot sizes and property types in Oregon City?

Oregon City spans everything from dense urban parcels in the historic core to large suburban lots in newer development areas like Beavercreek Road. We design for the specific scale and constraints of your property, not a one-size template.
