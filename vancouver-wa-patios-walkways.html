Common Questions

Patios & Walkways in Vancouver, WA — FAQ

Do patio impervious surface rules apply in Vancouver differently than Portland?

Vancouver and Clark County have their own stormwater management requirements, which are similar in intent to Oregon's but administered separately. We assess your lot coverage and design drainage to meet applicable Clark County standards.

What paving materials hold up best in Vancouver's climate?

Vancouver's climate is essentially the same as Portland's — wet winters, occasional freeze events, dry summers. Concrete pavers are the most durable, cost-effective choice. Natural stone and travertine are popular premium upgrades with comparable durability.

How does Washington State code apply to patio and walkway construction?

Washington State Building Code governs structural elements — steps, walls over certain heights, and covered structures adjacent to patios. We're experienced with both Oregon and Washington requirements so your project is code-compliant regardless of which side of the river you're on.
