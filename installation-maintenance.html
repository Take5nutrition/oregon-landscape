Installation & Maintenance

Built by Our Crew. Maintained by Our Crew.

Oregon Landscape never subcontracts. Every project — from the first excavation to the final plant — is executed by our own trained, in-house team. That means consistent quality, clear accountability, and crews who take pride in the work because their name is on it.

We also offer ongoing maintenance programs for the landscapes we build and for existing properties that need a skilled crew to stay sharp. Seasonal cleanups, pruning, mulching, irrigation adjustments — done on a regular schedule so nothing falls behind.

Whether you need us for a one-time installation or as a regular partner in keeping your property looking right, we're the same team doing the same quality work.

What We Do

Installation & Maintenance Services

Full-Property Installation

Complete landscape installation from ground-up — grading, hardscape, planting, lawn, irrigation, and lighting all managed by one crew from one company.

Scheduled Maintenance Programs

Monthly, bi-monthly, or seasonal maintenance visits — mowing, edging, pruning, mulching, irrigation checks, and seasonal color rotations on a consistent schedule.

Pruning & Trimming

Ornamental trees and shrubs pruned for shape, health, and the right structure at the right time of year — not just hacked back to keep things in bounds.

Spring & Fall Cleanups

Debris removal, bed edging, mulch refresh, and seasonal prep — in spring to get everything ready for the growing season, in fall to protect plants through winter.

Irrigation Seasonal Service

Spring startup, summer adjustments, and fall blowout — keep your irrigation system running efficiently and protected through freeze cycles.

Workmanship Warranty

Every installation is backed by our workmanship guarantee. If something we built fails due to installation error, we come back and make it right — no questions asked.
