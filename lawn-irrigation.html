Quality lawn installation and custom irrigation systems — built for Oregon's wet winters and dry summers.

Lawn Installation

A Great Lawn Starts With the Right Foundation

A healthy, durable lawn is built from the ground up — and Oregon Landscape does it right every time. We begin with a site analysis to assess soil composition, sun exposure, drainage patterns, and slope before selecting the right turf variety for your specific conditions.

We prepare the site with proper grading, topsoil amendment, and weed suppression before installing quality sod or seeding. Sprinkler systems are planned and installed alongside the lawn so everything works together from day one.

We also design and install standalone irrigation systems for existing lawns, planting beds, and entire properties — pressure-tested and calibrated for efficient, uniform coverage without waste.

Lawn & Irrigation Services

Site Analysis & Soil Prep

Every lawn installation begins with a site assessment — soil testing, grading review, and sun/shade mapping — so we select the right grass and prepare the ground properly before a single roll of sod is laid.

Sod Installation

Premium sod varieties selected for Oregon conditions — fescue blends for shade and moderate drought tolerance, ryegrass blends for high-traffic areas. Properly rolled, jointed, and edged.

Slopes & Difficult Areas

Steep slopes, narrow strips, and oddly shaped areas handled with the right combination of sod, erosion control blankets, and jute netting as needed to keep things in place.

Irrigation System Design

Custom sprinkler system layouts designed around your property's pressure, coverage zones, and plant water needs — planned before installation for maximum efficiency.

Sprinkler Installation

Pop-up sprinkler heads, rotors, drip zones, and backflow preventers installed cleanly and pressure-tested before covering. Wired to a programmable smart controller.

Repairs & System Maintenance

Broken heads, leaking valves, controller failures, and seasonal startup/shutdown — we service all brands and keep your system running efficiently year after year.
